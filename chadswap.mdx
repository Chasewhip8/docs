## MEV Protection

Protection against MEV is enabled by default on ChadSwap. This means that your transactions are protected from front-running bots that can manipulate the price of your swaps. This ia acheived by using Helius stake weighted connections.

## Fees

ChadSwap charges a 0.01 SOL fee for every swap that has a minimum output value of $1 to the Based Treasury.
